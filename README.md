🍳 ChefCart – Book a Chef at Home

ChefCart is a mobile application that allows users to book professional chefs to cook meals at their homes. The platform connects customers with skilled chefs who can prepare customized meals based on user preferences, making home dining easier and more convenient.

This application helps people enjoy freshly cooked, restaurant-style food at home without the need to cook themselves.

📱 Project Overview

ChefCart is designed to solve the problem of finding skilled cooks for home meals, small events, or special occasions. Users can browse chefs, view their specialties, choose dishes, and book cooking services directly from the app.

The platform creates a bridge between customers and professional chefs.
